Contact Us

Thank you for visiting SalaryToolsMY. If you have feedback about our calculators or guides, you are welcome to contact us by email. We read user messages to improve the clarity and usefulness of our Malaysia salary content.

Email

salarytoolsmy@proton.me

Contact form (placeholder)

A full web contact form may be added in future updates. For now, email is the primary contact channel. If you email us, include a short and clear subject line so we can understand your request quickly (for example: “Calculator suggestion” or “Guide correction request”).

What you can contact us for

  • Suggestions to improve salary, EPF, SOCSO, EIS or PCB calculator usability
  • Requests to correct factual wording in a guide page
  • General website feedback, including unclear explanations or broken links
  • General enquiries about how to use SalaryToolsMY pages for salary planning

What you should NOT send

For privacy and safety, please do not send sensitive personal or employer-confidential information. This includes:

  • IC number or passport number
  • Full payslip documents
  • Bank account or card details
  • Tax file number or complete tax return data
  • Employer confidential payroll files or internal HR records

Response expectations

We try to review incoming messages, but replies are not guaranteed and may take time. Messages sent to SalaryToolsMY do not create a professional advisory relationship. Any response is general in nature and cannot replace advice from qualified payroll, tax, legal or financial professionals.

Short disclaimer

SalaryToolsMY does not provide payroll, tax, legal or financial services. All website content is educational and estimate-based.
