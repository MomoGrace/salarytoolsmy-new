How to Calculate Salary After EPF, SOCSO and PCB in Malaysia

Learn the basic salary deduction formula used to estimate Malaysian take-home pay.

To calculate salary after EPF, SOCSO and PCB in Malaysia, start with your gross salary and subtract employee deductions. These usually include EPF, SOCSO, EIS and PCB.

Basic formula

Net salary = Gross salary - EPF - SOCSO - EIS - PCB - other payroll deductions

Step-by-step calculation

StepWhat to do
1Start with gross monthly salary
2Subtract employee EPF contribution
3Subtract SOCSO and EIS
4Subtract PCB / MTD if applicable
5The balance is estimated take-home pay

Why manual calculation may not match payroll exactly

Payroll calculation may include tax reliefs, bonuses, allowances, unpaid leave, overtime, benefits, zakat and company-specific deductions. That is why estimates should be used for planning only.

FAQ

Is PCB the same as income tax?

PCB is a monthly tax deduction. It is used as instalment payment toward income tax based on estimated annual income.

Can take-home pay change every month?

Yes. It can change due to overtime, bonus, unpaid leave, allowances, claims, tax changes or other payroll adjustments.
