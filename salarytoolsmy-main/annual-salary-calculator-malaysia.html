About SalaryToolsMY

SalaryToolsMY provides free salary and payroll estimation tools for users in Malaysia. The site is designed to help employees, job seekers, freelancers and small business owners quickly understand estimated salary deductions and take-home pay.

What we provide

  • Salary and net pay estimators
  • EPF, SOCSO and EIS estimation tools
  • PCB, income tax, overtime and bonus calculators
  • Simple guides and examples for salary planning

Our goal

Our goal is to make salary planning easier to understand. The tools are simplified and intended for general planning only, not official payroll, tax or legal advice.
