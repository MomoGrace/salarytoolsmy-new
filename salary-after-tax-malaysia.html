Salary After Tax Malaysia

Salary after tax is the amount left after estimated tax and payroll deductions. In Malaysia, PCB may be deducted monthly depending on income and payroll settings.

What affects after-tax salary?

  • Annual income
  • Tax reliefs
  • PCB deductions
  • EPF, SOCSO and EIS
