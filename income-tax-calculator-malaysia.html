How this income tax calculator works

This tool estimates taxable income after basic relief entries and applies a simplified progressive tax estimate for planning. Actual income tax depends on resident status, reliefs, rebates, benefits, deductions and official tax rules.

Use this with PCB planning

Annual income tax and monthly PCB are related, but they are not always the same. PCB is deducted monthly, while final tax is confirmed through annual tax filing.

Income tax calculator FAQ

Can I use this for final tax filing?

No. Use official LHDN systems or a tax professional for final filing.
